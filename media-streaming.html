Competition: Design Your Media Streamer

Design and Development of a media streaming server

What do you need to do

  • need to choose from a static audio or video resource (files sitting in the server) as on demand.....
  • need to choose the format, resolution and dimension based on the device that will play the stream....
  • from the same feed a mobile device will choose smaller size video stream but a desktop with larger screen and higher bandwidth will choose a different stream to balance the response time, quality of user experience based on the device someone is watching it from.....

Rules

  • Open to 2nd year undergraduate students
  • Group of up to 3 individual members (single person group is possible)
  • Need to demonstrate, write a short report / doc, provide code
