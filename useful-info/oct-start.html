Week sturcture

Week 1: Set up tools and start reading literature.
Week 2-3: Write introduction and background.
Week 4-5: Write state-of-the-art.
Week 6: Complete state-of-the-art and plan for implementation (design, structure etc.)
Week 7-10: Implementation.
Week 11: Initial results and design review.
Week 12-13: Improve implementation and produce final results.
Week 14: Write methodology, results & Discussions.
Week 15: Write abstract, conclusions and revise all previously written sections.
Week 16: Improvements and final thesis.
